About Me

My name is Thomas Wood and I am an enthusiastic web developer. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Construction Science from the University of Oklahoma in 2013, and began my professional career in commercial construction management. I began in the field, working as a Field Engineer on the University of Texas' Dell Medical School Research Facility in Austin, TX. I went through the ranks, ending my corporate career in Oklahoma as a Project Manager. I then started my own business called Wood's Construction Services, and later moved to Houston, TX.

While my background has been primarily in construction, I have always had a passion for technology. At the age of 14, I saved up money by working in my father's auto parts warehouse and I built my first computer. I used this computer to play my favorite video games and chat with fellow gamers via website forums. Through these forums, I was first introduced to HTML. While I regret not pursuing web development any further than HTML until recently, I value my time spent in construction management because it gave me the ambition and leadership that will prove valuable in any career. I hope you enjoy my personal website and feel free to contact me with any comments or questions.
